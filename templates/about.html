We are a team of 8 Informatics and Public Health students at the University of California, Irvine. Our goal is to simplify and streamline the process of getting your health data into your hands, all while increasing understanding. For decades, the healthcare industry has struggled to standardize the way they track patient records. Although some attempts have been made, significant progress has still not been seen. With the advent of electronic records, we are becoming able to parse through data in order to organize it in a user-friendly way. The key feature of this project is the development of a database and logic engine which will identify key metrics in a patient’s aggregated record, then explain their significance and levels in regards to the individual’s record. Each number will be shown as good, bad, or normal, and why.
